Allan Jonson

Using a variety of psychotherapy and coaching techniques, Allan can help you regain a sense of confidence and enjoyment in life; live up to your full potential and realize your dreams. This is a chance to change the habits you don’t like and bring joy back into your life. Psychotherapy services are provided for those suffering from depression, anxiety, relationship difficulties, grief or bereavement, lack of self-confidence, stress-related problems, or major life transitions (divorce, career change, etc.) as well as personal development work.
